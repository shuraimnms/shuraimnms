Travel Dhikr

traveling dhikr

Dua 1: Before Starting the Journey

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي تَوَكَّلتُ عَلَيْكَ وَفَوَّضْتُ أَمْرِي إِلَيْكَ

Allahumma inni tawakkaltu 'alayka wa fawwadh-tu amri ilayk

"O Allah, I place my trust in You and I entrust my affairs to You."

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6369

Dua 2: For a Safe Journey

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنينَ وَإِنَّا إِلَى رَبِّنَا لَمُنقَلِبُونَ

Subhana allathee sakhkhara lana hatha wa ma kunna lahoo muqreeneen, wa inna ila rabbina lamunqaliboon

"Glory is to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could never have done it by ourselves, and to our Lord is our final return."

Reference: Quran, Surah Az-Zukhruf 43:13-14

Dua 3: For Protection During Travel

اللَّهُمَّ احْفَظْنِي مِنْ بَيْنِ يَدَيَّ وَمِنْ خَلْفِي وَعَنْ يَمِينِي وَعَنْ شِمَالِي وَمِنْ فَوْقِي وَأَعُوذُ بِعَظَمَتِكَ أَنْ أُغْتَالَ مِنْ تَحْتِي

Allahumma ihfazni min bayni yadayya wa min khalfii wa ‘an yameeni wa ‘an shimali wa min fawqi wa a’udhu bi’azamatika an ughtaal min tahtii

"O Allah, protect me from in front of me, from behind me, from my right, from my left, and from above me, and I seek refuge in Your greatness from being attacked from below me."

Reference: Sahih Muslim 589

Dua 4: For a Smooth Journey

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْهُ رِحْلَةً مَبْرُورَةً وَسَعْيًَا مَشْكُورًا وَذَنْبًا مَغْفُورًا

Allahumma ajilhu rihlatan mabrooran wa sa’iyan mashkooran wa dhanban magfooran

"O Allah, make this journey one that is blessed, with thanks for my efforts, and forgive my sins."

Reference: Sunan al-Tirmidhi 3576

Dua 5: For Returning Home Safely

أَوَّلُهَا السَّلاَمُ وَآخِرُهَا السَّلاَمُ

Awwaluha as-salamu wa akhiruha as-salamu

"The first of it is peace, and the last of it is peace."

Reference: Sahih Muslim 1739
