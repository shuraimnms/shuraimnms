Forgiveness Dhikr

Dua 1: After Salah

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ . (ثَلاثاً) اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ السَّلاَمُ، وَمِنْكَ السَّلَامُ، تَبَارَكْتَ يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالإِكْرَامِ
(3X)

Astaghfiru-llaha (thalaathaa) Allahumma Anta-s-Salaamu, wa minka-s-salaamu, tabaarakta ya dha-l-jalaali wa-l-ikraam

"I seek forgiveness from Allah (three times). O Allah, You are the Peace, and from You comes peace. Blessed are You, O Possessor of majesty and honor."

Reference: Muslim 2699

Dua 2: For Forgiveness

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ الَّذِي لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ
(1X)

Astaghfirullaha alladhi laa ilaaha illa huwa al-Hayyul-Qayyoomu wa atoobu ilayh

"I seek forgiveness from Allah, there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer, and I turn to Him in repentance."

Reference: Sunan Abi Dawood 1517

Dua 3: Morning and Evening

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَتُبْ عَلَيَّ إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ
(3X)

Rabbighfir li watub ‘alayya innaka anta at-tawwabur rahim

"My Lord, forgive me and accept my repentance. Indeed, You are the Most-Relenting, the Most Merciful."

Reference: At-Tirmidhi 3434

Dua 4: Glorifying Allah

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ، أَسْتَغْفِرُهُ
(100X in a day)

SubhanAllahi wa bihamdi, astaghfiruhu

"Glory is to Allah, and praise is to Him. I seek His forgiveness."

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6405

Dua 5: Morning Supplication

اللّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ أَنْتَ، خَلَقْتَنِي وَأَنَا عَبْدُكَ
(Morning & Evening - 1X)

Allahumma anta Rabbi la ilaha illa anta, khalaqtani wa ana ‘abduka

"O Allah, You are my Lord. There is no deity except You. You created me, and I am Your servant."

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6306
