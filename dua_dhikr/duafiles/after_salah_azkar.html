نماز کے بعد ذکر
After Salah Dhikr

Dua After Namaz

Dua 1

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ . (ثَلاثاً) اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ السَّلاَمُ، وَمِنْكَ السَّلَامُ، تَبَارَكْتَ يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالإِكْرَامِ
(3X)

Astaghfiru-llaha (thalaathaa) Allahumma Anta-s-Salaamu, wa minka-s-salaamu, tabaarakta ya dha-l-jalaali wa-l-ikraam

"I seek forgiveness from Allah (three times). O Allah, You are the Peace, and from You comes peace. Blessed are You, O Possessor of majesty and honor"

Reference: Muslim 2699

Dua 2

لَا إلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
(1X)

La ilaha illa-llahu wahdahu la sharika lahu, lahu-l-mulku wa lahu-l-hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir

"There is no god but Allah, alone, without any partner. To Him belongs the kingdom and to Him belongs all praise, and He is capable of everything"

Reference: Muslim 2699

Dua 3

اللَّهُمَّ لَا مَانِعَ لِمَا أَعْطَيْتَ، وَلَا مُعْطِيَ لِمَا مَنَعْتَ، وَلَا يَنْفَعُ ذَا الْجَدِّ مِنْكَ الْجَدُّ
(1X)

Allahumma la mani'a lima a'tayta, wa la mu'tiya lima mana'ta, wa la yanfa'u dha-l-jaddi minka-l-jadd

"O Allah, none can withhold what You have given, and none can give what You have withheld, and the riches of the rich do not benefit them against You"

Reference: Muslim 2699

Dua 4

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ.
(34X) (33x) (33x)

Subhana-llahi, wa-l-hamdu li-llahi, wa-llahu akbar (thalaathaa wa thalaathin)

"Glory be to Allah, and all praise is due to Allah, and Allah is the greatest (33 times)"

Reference: Muslim 2699

Dua 5

لَا إِلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
(1X)

La ilaha illa-llahu wahdahu la sharika lahu, lahu-l-mulku wa lahu-l-hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir

"There is no god but Allah, alone, without any partner. To Him belongs the kingdom and to Him belongs all praise, and He is capable of everything"

Reference: Muslim 2699

Surah Al-Ikhlas

بِسْمِ ٱللهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ قُلْ هُوَ ٱللهُ أَحَدٌ ٱللهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ
(3X)

Bismi-llahi-r-Rahmani-r-Rahim Qul Huwa-llahu Ahad Allah-u-s-Samad Lam yalid wa lam yulad Wa lam yakun lahu kufuwan ahad

"In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. Say, He is Allah, the One. Allah, the Self-Sufficient Master. He begets not, nor was He begotten. And there is none co-equal or comparable unto Him."

Reference: Al-Ikhlas 112:1-4

Surah Al-Falaq

بِسْمِ ٱللهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلْفَلَقِ مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّـٰثَـٰتِ فِى ٱلْعُقَدِ وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ
(3X)

Bismi-llahi-r-Rahmani-r-Rahim Qul a'udhu bi Rabb-i-l-Falaq Min sharri ma khalaq Wa min sharri ghasiqin idha waqab Wa min sharri-n-naffathati fi-l-'uqad Wa min sharri hasidin idha hasad

"In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. Say, I seek refuge in the Lord of the dawn. From the evil of what He has created. And from the evil of the darkening (night) when it comes. And from the evil of the witches who blow on the knots. And from the evil of the envier when he envies."

Reference: Al-Falaq 113:1-5

Surah An-Nas

بِسْمِ ٱللهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ إِلَـٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ مِن شَرِّ ٱلْوَسْوَاسِ ٱلْخَنَّاسِ ٱلَّذِى يُوَسْوِسُ فِى صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ مِنَ ٱلْجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ
(3X)

Bismi-llahi-r-Rahmani-r-Rahim Qul a'udhu bi Rabb-i-n-Nas Maliki-n-Nas Ilahi-n-Nas Min sharri-l-waswasi-l-khannas Al-ladhi yuwaswisu fi suduri-n-Nas Mina-l-jinnati wa-n-Nas

"In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. Say, I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind. The King of mankind. The God of mankind. From the evil of the whisperer who withdraws. Who whispers in the breasts of mankind. From among the jinn and mankind."

Reference: An-Nas 114:1-6

Ayatul Kursi

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ لَا تَأْخُذُهُ سِنَةٌ وَلَا نَوْمٌ لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ مَنْ ذَا الَّذِي يَشْفَعُ عِنْدَهُ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِ يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ وَلَا يُحِيطُونَ بِشَيْءٍ مِنْ عِلْمِهِ إِلَّا بِمَا شَاءَ وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ وَلَا يَئُودُهُ حِفْظُهُمَا وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيمُ
(1X)

Allahu la ilaha illa Huwa-l-Hayyu-l-Qayyum la ta'khudhuhu sinatu wa la nawm lahu ma fi-s-samawati wa ma fi-l-ardi man dha-l-ladhi yashfa'u 'indahu illa bi-idhnihi ya'lamu ma bayna aydihim wa ma khalfahum wa la yuhiytuna bi-shay'in min 'ilmihi illa bi-ma sha'a wa si'a kursiyyuhu-s-samawati wa-l-arda wa la ya'uduhu hifzuhuma wa Huwa-l-'Aliyyu-l-'Azim

"Allah! There is no god but He, the Living, the Self-Subsisting, Eternal. No slumber can seize Him nor sleep. His are all things in the heavens and on earth. Who is there can intercede in His presence except as He permitteth? He knoweth what (appeareth to His creatures as) before or after or behind them. Nor shall they compass aught of His knowledge except as He willeth. His Throne doth extend over the heavens and the earth, and He feeleth no fatigue in guarding and preserving them for He is the Most High, the Supreme (in glory)."

Reference: Al-Baqarah 2:255

Dua for Knowledge and Provision

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْماً نَافِعاً وَرِزْقاً طَيِّباً وَعَمَلاً مُتَقَبَّلاً
(1X)

Allahumma inni as'aluka 'ilman nafi'an wa rizqan tayyiban wa 'amalan mutaqabbalan

"O Allah, I ask You for knowledge that is beneficial, and provision that is good, and deeds that are accepted"

Reference: Muslim 2699
