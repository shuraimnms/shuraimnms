Protection Dhikr

Dua 1: Protection from Evil

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
(3X Morning & Evening)

Bismillahil-ladhi la yadhurru ma’asmihi shay’un fil-ardi wa la fis-sama’i wa huwa-s-Sami’ul-‘Aleem

"In the name of Allah with Whose name nothing in the earth or the heavens can harm, and He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing."

Reference: Abu Dawood 5088, At-Tirmidhi 3388

Dua 2: Seeking Refuge from Shaytan

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
(3X Morning & Evening)

A’udhu bikalimatillahi-tammati min sharri ma khalaq

"I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created."

Reference: Sahih Muslim 2708

Dua 3: Protection from Hellfire

اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ
(7X Morning & Evening)

Allahumma ajirni min an-Nar

"O Allah, save me from the Hellfire."

Reference: Abu Dawood 5079

Dua 4: Protection from Every Harm

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ
(7X Morning & Evening)

Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa huwa ‘alayhi tawakkaltu wa huwa Rabbul-‘Arshil-‘Adheem

"Allah is sufficient for me; there is no deity except Him. Upon Him, I have relied, and He is the Lord of the Great Throne."

Reference: Surah At-Tawbah 9:129

Dua 5: Seeking Allah’s Protection

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ
(Whenever needed)

A’udhu billahi min ash-Shaytan-ir-Rajim

"I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Shaytan."

Reference: Surah An-Nahl 16:98

Dua 6: Complete Protection

اللَّهُمَّ احْفَظْنِي مِنْ بَيْنِ يَدَيَّ وَمِنْ خَلْفِي وَعَنْ يَمِينِي وَعَنْ شِمَالِي وَمِنْ فَوْقِي وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ أَنْ أُغْتَالَ مِنْ تَحْتِي
(1X Morning & Evening)

Allahumma ihfadhni min bayni yadayya wa min khalfi wa ‘an yameeni wa ‘an shimali wa min fawqi wa a’udhu bika an ughtala min tahti

"O Allah, protect me from in front of me, behind me, on my right, on my left, and from above me. I seek refuge in You from being taken unaware from beneath me."

Reference: Abu Dawood 5074

Dua 7: Protection from Every Evil

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
(3X Morning & Evening)

Bismillahil-ladhi la yadhurru ma’asmihi shay’un fil-ardi wa la fis-sama’i wa huwa-s-Sami’ul-‘Aleem

"In the name of Allah, who does not harm anyone in the earth or in the sky. He is the All-Hearing, the All-Wise."

Reference: Abu Dawood 5088, At-Tirmidhi 3388, Ibn Majah 4168

Dua 8: Protection from the Evil Eye

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْطَانٍ وَهَامَّةٍ وَمِنْ كُلِّ عَيْنٍ لَامَّةٍ
(3X Morning & Evening)

A’udhu bika-limata-l-lahi-lta’amat min kulli shay’tanin wa hammati min kulli ‘ayni lammatin

"I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from every devil, harmful creature, and evil eye."

Reference: Surah Al-Baqarah 28:22

Dua 9: Protection from the Evil

اللَهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ

Allahumma inni as-Suluk min

"O Allah, I ask You for help."

Reference: Surah Al-Hijr 10:15
