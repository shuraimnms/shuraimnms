📱 What is ZikrMate?

ZikrMate is a powerful **Islamic app** designed to help Muslims **learn, recite, and memorize** daily duas and azkar.

✨ Features

  • ✅ **Duas & Azkar:** Includes Hisnul Muslim, Morning & Evening Azkar
  • ✅ **Quran Recitation:** Listen to beautiful recitations
  • ✅ **Themes:** Customize the app's look and feel
  • ✅ **Offline Mode:** Use the app even without internet
  • ✅ **Favorites & Folders:** Save important duas for quick access
  • ✅ **Simple UI:** Clean and easy-to-use design

📖 How to Use?

  • 🟢 **Browse:** Navigate through different categories of duas
  • 🟢 **Listen:** Play audio recitations for better learning
  • 🟢 **Bookmark:** Save your favorite duas for quick access
  • 🟢 **Customize:** Switch themes and font sizes for comfort
