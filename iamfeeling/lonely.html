Dua for Loneliness / تنہائی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي فِي مَرَحَمَتِكَ

"Allahumma ajirni fi rahmatika"

Translation: "O Allah, grant me comfort in Your mercy."

Explanation: This dua is recited when feeling lonely, seeking Allah’s mercy and comfort to ease the feelings of isolation and to remind us that He is always near.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Comfort and Solitude
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whenever you feel alone or isolated, remember that Allah is always with you." (Tirmidhi)
