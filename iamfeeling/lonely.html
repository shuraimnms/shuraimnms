Dua for Loneliness / تنہائی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ صَاحِبِي فِي السَّفَرِ وَالْخَلِيفَةُ فِي الْأَهْلِ

"Allahumma Anta sahibi fis-safar, wal-khalifatu fil-ahl."

Translation: "O Allah, You are my companion in solitude, and my guardian in all affairs."

Explanation: This dua reminds us that even in loneliness, Allah is always with us. It was recited by the Prophet (ﷺ) during travel, but it can also be said in times of solitude.

Hadith Reference: This dua is mentioned in *Hisnul Muslim*, and the Prophet (ﷺ) used to say it when feeling distant or alone.
