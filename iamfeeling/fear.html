Dua for Fear / خوف کے وقت کی دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ الْهَمِّ وَالْحُزْنِ وَالْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ

"Allahumma inni a’udhu bika minal-hammi wal-huzni wal-‘ajzi wal-kasali."

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety, grief, weakness, and laziness."

Hadith Reference: (Sahih Bukhari: 6369)
