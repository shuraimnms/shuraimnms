Dua for Gratitude / شکر گزار ہونے کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ نِعْمَةٍ

"Allahumma laka al-hamdu ‘ala kulli ni’mah"

Translation: "O Allah, all praise is for You for every blessing."

Explanation: This dua is a way of expressing gratitude to Allah for all the blessings He has granted. It encourages recognizing and appreciating the countless favors bestowed upon us.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Gratitude
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whoever is not grateful to people, is not grateful to Allah." (Sunan Abu Dawood)
