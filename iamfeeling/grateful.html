Dua for Gratitude / شکر گزار ہونے کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ مَا أَصْبَحَ بِي مِنْ نِعْمَةٍ أَوْ بِأَحَدٍ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ فَمِنْكَ وَحْدَكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ، فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ وَلَكَ الشُّكْرُ

"Allahumma ma asbaha bi min ni'matin aw bi ahadin min khalqika faminka wahdaka la sharika laka, falakal-hamdu walakash-shukr."

Translation: "O Allah, whatever blessing has been received by me or anyone of Your creation is from You alone, You have no partner. All praise is for You and thanks is to You."

Explanation: This supplication acknowledges that all blessings, whether upon oneself or others, originate solely from Allah. It is a comprehensive expression of gratitude, recognizing His unparalleled generosity and affirming that all praise and thanks are due to Him alone.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whoever recites this in the morning has completed his obligation to thank Allah for that day; and whoever says it in the evening has completed his obligation for that night." (Abu Dawud)
