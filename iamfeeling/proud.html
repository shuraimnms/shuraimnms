Dua for Pride / تکبر کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي تَوَاضُعًا لِكُلِّ مُسْلِمٍ

"Allahumma ajil fi qalbi tawadu'an likulli muslimin"

Translation: "O Allah, place humility in my heart towards every Muslim."

Explanation: This dua is recited to ask Allah to remove arrogance and replace it with humility. It is a reminder that all pride is a form of arrogance and we should remain humble before Allah and His creation.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Humility
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said, "Whoever has an atom's weight of pride in his heart will not enter Jannah." (Sahih Muslim)
