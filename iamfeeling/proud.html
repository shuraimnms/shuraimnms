Dua for Humility / تکبر سے بچنے کی دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي فِي عَيْنِي صَغِيرًا وَفِي أَعْيُنِ النَّاسِ كَبِيرًا

"Allahumma aj‘alni fi ‘ayni sagheeran wa fi a‘yunin-naasi kabeeran."

Translation: "O Allah, make me small in my own eyes, and great in the eyes of people."

Explanation: This dua teaches humility and reminds us to stay grounded. It asks Allah to keep us from arrogance while allowing us to earn respect among people.

Hadith Reference: This dua is found in *Hisnul Muslim* and aligns with the Prophet’s (ﷺ) teachings on avoiding arrogance.
