Dua for Anger / غصہ کے لئے دعا

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

"A'udhu billahi min ash-shaytan ir-rajim"

Translation: "I seek refuge with Allah from the accursed devil."

Explanation: Anger is a natural emotion, but it should be controlled. This dua seeks Allah's refuge from the devil, who might inspire anger in us. Reciting this dua helps in calming down and regaining control over one's emotions.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Anger
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "If any of you feels angry, let him perform wudu (ablution)." (Sahih Bukhari)
