Dua for Anger / غصہ کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي وَأَذْهِبْ غَيْظَ قَلْبِي وَأَجِرْنِي مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

"Allahummaghfir li dhanbi wa adhhhib ghaydha qalbi wa ajirni min ash-shaytan ir-rajim."

Translation: "O Allah, forgive my sin, remove the anger from my heart, and protect me from the accursed devil."

Explanation: This dua asks Allah for forgiveness, to remove anger from the heart, and to protect from the whispers of Shaytan. It is a powerful supplication to calm oneself during moments of anger.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Anger
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) advised saying this dua and performing wudu (ablution) when feeling angry. (Sahih Bukhari & Hisnul Muslim)
