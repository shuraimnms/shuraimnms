Dua for Anxiety / پریشانی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحُزْنِ

"Allahumma inni a’udhu bika minal hammi wal huzn"

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and sorrow."

Explanation: This dua is recited to seek relief from anxiety, worry, and sorrow. It is a reminder to place trust in Allah for ease and tranquility during difficult moments.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Relief from Anxiety and Sorrow
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whoever says this dua, Allah will relieve their worry and sadness." (Sahih al-Bukhari)
