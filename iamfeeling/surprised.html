Dua for Surprise / حیرانی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ أَفْرِحْ قَلْبِي بِمَا فَجَأَنِي

"Allahumma afrikh qalbi bima faja'nī"

Translation: "O Allah, fill my heart with joy for what has surprised me."

Explanation: This dua is recited when someone experiences surprise, asking Allah to fill their heart with happiness and joy even in unexpected moments.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Joy and Surprise
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) taught us to seek Allah’s blessings in moments of unexpected joy and surprise, acknowledging that everything happens by Allah's will.
