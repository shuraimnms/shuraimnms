Dua for Surprise / حیرت کے وقت کی دعا

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ

"SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi."

Translation: "Glory is to Allah and all praise is to Him."

Explanation: This dua is a Sunnah way to express amazement when witnessing something astonishing, whether good or unexpected.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) frequently recited *SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi*, especially when amazed by Allah’s creation. (Sahih Muslim)
