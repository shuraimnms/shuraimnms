Dua for Sadness / غم کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْحُزْنِ

"Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal huzn"

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from sorrow."

Explanation: This dua is recited when one feels overwhelmed with sadness or grief. It is a way to seek Allah's help in times of emotional distress. Reciting this dua helps to bring peace and comfort.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Sadness
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whoever is feeling sad or distressed, let him recite this dua for comfort." (Sunan Ibn Majah)
