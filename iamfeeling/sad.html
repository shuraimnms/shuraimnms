Dua for Sadness / غم کے وقت کی دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحُزْنِ

"Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika minal-hammi wal-huzn."

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and grief."

Explanation: This dua helps in times of sadness and anxiety, asking Allah for relief from sorrow and distress.

Hadith Reference: This dua is mentioned in *Hisnul Muslim* and is a supplication the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) used for seeking relief from grief.
