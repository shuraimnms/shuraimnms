Dua for Tiredness / تھکاوٹ کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ يَتْعَبُنِي

"Allahumma inni a'udhu bika min kulli shay'in yat'abuni"

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from everything that tires me."

Explanation: This dua is recited when feeling fatigued or exhausted. It is a reminder to seek Allah’s assistance and find peace in His presence during times of tiredness.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Relief from Tiredness
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) advised seeking refuge in Allah from all hardships and exhaustion, as Allah is the ultimate source of rest and comfort.
