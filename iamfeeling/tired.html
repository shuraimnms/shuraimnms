Dua for Tiredness / تھکن کے وقت کی دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ

"Allahumma inni a’udhu bika minal ‘ajzi wal kasal."

Translation: "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from weakness and laziness."

Explanation: This dua is a Sunnah way to ask Allah for strength, energy, and protection from tiredness or exhaustion.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) often recited this dua to seek protection from fatigue and laziness. (Sahih Muslim)
