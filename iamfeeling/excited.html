Dua for Excitement / جوش کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ فَرَحًا لَا يَفْنَى وَسُرُورًا لَا يَزُولُ

"Allahumma inni as'aluka farahan laa yafnaa wa surooran laa yazool."

Translation: "O Allah, I ask You for joy that never fades and happiness that never ends."

Explanation: This supplication is a beautiful way to ask Allah for enduring happiness and lasting joy. It reflects the believer’s desire to experience excitement and contentment in both this life and the hereafter.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) would often make duas asking for happiness and protection from sorrow, guiding believers to seek joy through remembrance of Allah. (Hisnul Muslim)
