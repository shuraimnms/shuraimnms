Dua for Excitement / جوش کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي فَرَحًا

"Allahumma ajil fi qalbi farahan"

Translation: "O Allah, place joy in my heart."

Explanation: This dua is recited to ask Allah to bring joy and excitement into one's heart, particularly when looking forward to something important or when feeling elated.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Joy and Excitement
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) encouraged seeking Allah’s blessings in all states, including moments of joy and excitement.
