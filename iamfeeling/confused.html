Dua for Confusion / الجھن کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اَفْهِمْنِي مَا أَشْكُرُهُ

"Allahumma afhimni ma ashkuruh"

Translation: "O Allah, make me understand what I am confused about."

Explanation: This dua is recited when one feels confused or is in need of guidance to make the right decisions. It asks Allah for clarity and understanding.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Guidance and Understanding
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) urged seeking Allah's guidance, especially in times of confusion or uncertainty.
