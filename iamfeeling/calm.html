Dua for Calm / سکون کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي سَكِينَةً

"Allahumma ajil fi qalbi sakinah"

Translation: "O Allah, place tranquility in my heart."

Explanation: This dua is recited to seek calmness, peace, and tranquility from Allah, especially during moments of anxiety or stress.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Peace and Calmness
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) encouraged seeking Allah’s help in achieving inner peace and calm during times of turmoil.
