Dua for Hope / امید کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الرَّجَاءَ فِي كُلِّ خَيْرٍ

"Allahumma inni as’aluka ar-rajaa’a fi kulli khayr."

Translation: "O Allah, I ask You for hope in all that is good."

Explanation: This dua asks Allah to grant us hope and optimism in all situations, reminding us that Allah's mercy and blessings are always near.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "No one should lose hope in Allah’s mercy, for His mercy encompasses all things." (Hisnul Muslim)
