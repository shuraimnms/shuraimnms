Dua for Hope / امید کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي أَمَلًا

"Allahumma ajil fi qalbi amalan"

Translation: "O Allah, place hope in my heart."

Explanation: This dua is recited to ask Allah to instill hope in the heart, especially in challenging situations. It serves as a reminder that hope in Allah is a source of strength during difficult times.

Hadith Name: The Dua for Hope
Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) encouraged placing hope in Allah, as He is the ultimate source of all help and support.
