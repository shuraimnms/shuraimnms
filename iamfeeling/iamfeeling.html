How Are You Feeling Today?

Happy/خوش

Feeling joy and contentment. Life is good!

خوشی - خوشی اور اطمینان کا احساس۔ زندگی اچھی ہے!

This feeling is often associated with happiness and satisfaction. It's the joy that comes when everything feels right.

Angry/ناراض

Frustration or annoyance. Need to let off some steam!

غصہ - مایوسی یا ناراضگی۔ غصہ کم کرنے کی ضرورت ہے!

This feeling usually arises when something or someone causes frustration. It's a normal emotional response, but it should be managed carefully.

Sad/اداس

Feeling down or sorrowful. Need some comfort.

اداس - نیچے یا غمگین محسوس کرنا۔ کچھ تسلی کی ضرورت ہے۔

This feeling is characterized by a sense of sorrow or melancholy. It's important to take care of yourself during these times.

Excited/پرجوش

Feeling enthusiastic and full of energy. Let’s go!

پر جوش - جوش و جذبہ اور توانائی سے بھرپور محسوس کرنا۔ چلیں!

Excitement is a feeling of high energy, often due to anticipation or enthusiasm. It's an exhilarating emotional state!

Calm/پرسکون

At peace and collected. The mind is at ease.

پرامن - سکون اور اطمینان محسوس کرنا۔ دماغ پرسکون ہے۔

This feeling is associated with tranquility and mental peace. It's a calm state of being where everything feels in balance.

Confused/کنفیوزڈ

Uncertain and unclear. Need some clarity.

مبہم - غیر یقینی اور غیر واضح محسوس کرنا۔ کچھ وضاحت کی ضرورت ہے۔

This feeling comes when one is unsure or lacks clarity. It's a state of confusion that can be resolved by gaining more information.

Surprised/حیران

Caught off guard! Something unexpected happened.

حیران - غافل ہو جانا! کچھ غیر متوقع ہوا۔

Surprise occurs when something unexpected happens, catching you off guard and leaving you in awe or astonishment.

Grateful/شکر گزار

Feeling thankful for the blessings in life.

شکر گزار - زندگی کی نعمتوں کے لئے شکر گزار محسوس کرنا۔

Gratitude is a feeling of appreciation and thankfulness, often arising when you acknowledge the blessings in your life.

Fear/ڈر

Feeling scared or anxious. Need reassurance.

ڈر - ڈر یا بے چینی محسوس کرنا۔ تصدیق کی ضرورت ہے۔

Fear is a natural response to danger or threat. It's important to seek comfort and reassurance during fearful moments.

Hope/امید

Feeling optimistic and positive about the future.

امید - مستقبل کے بارے میں امید و شاعرانہ محسوس کرنا۔

Hope is a feeling of positivity and expectation for good things to come. It's a belief in a brighter future.

Pride/فخر

Feeling proud of oneself or others. Celebrating achievements.

فخر - خود یا دوسروں پر فخر کرنا۔ کامیابیوں کی تقریب منانا۔

Pride is a feeling of satisfaction and accomplishment. It often comes from recognizing achievements and celebrating success.

Patience/صبر

Feeling calm and composed in challenging situations.

صبر - مشکل حالات میں سکون اور مضبوط محسوس کرنا۔

Patience is the ability to remain calm and composed in difficult situations. It's a virtue that helps you navigate challenges with grace.

Feeling strong and resilient in difficult situations.

Strength is the ability to withstand hardship and resilience in the face of adversity. It's a vital attribute for maintaining resilience in challenging situations.

Relief from Sorrow/غم سے نجات

Seeking comfort and solace during times of sadness.

غم سے نجات - غم کے وقت تسلی اور سکون کی تلاش۔

Relief from sorrow is a feeling of seeking comfort and solace during times of sadness or grief. It's important to find support and healing during difficult moments.

Lonely/تنہا

Feeling isolated and disconnected. Seeking company.

تنہا - الگ تھلگ اور بے جوڑ محسوس کرنا۔ ساتھی کی تلاش میں۔

Loneliness often arises when we feel disconnected from others. It’s a longing for social connection and companionship.

Dua for Inner Peace/داخلی امن کی دعا

Seeking tranquility and calmness within.

داخلی امن کی تلاش - اندر سکون اور سکون کی تلاش۔

The Dua for Inner Peace is recited to seek tranquility and calmness within oneself. It's a prayer for emotional and spiritual well-being.

Hopeful/امید مند

Looking forward to something better, full of hope.

مستقبل کے لئے امید بھرا دیکھنا، بہت سی امیدوں سے بھرا۔

Hope is a positive and uplifting feeling where you look forward to better outcomes, often with optimism and faith in the future.

Tired/تھکا ہوا

Feeling drained and low on energy. Need some rest.

تھکاوٹ - توانائی ختم ہونا اور آرام کی ضرورت ہونا۔

Being tired is a physical and mental state of exhaustion. It calls for rest and recovery to restore energy and vitality.

Courage/ہمت

Feeling brave and strong in the face of fear.

ہمت - خوف کے سامنے بہادر اور مضبوط محسوس کرنا۔

Courage is the ability to face fear and adversity with bravery and strength. It's a quality that empowers you to overcome challenges.

Proud/فخر

Feeling accomplished and proud of achievements.

فخر - کامیابیوں پر فخر محسوس کرنا۔

Pride comes from a sense of achievement and accomplishment. It is often felt after reaching a significant milestone or goal.

Anxious/بے چین

Feeling uneasy or worried. Need to calm down.

تشویش - بے چینی یا فکر کرنا۔ سکون کی ضرورت ہے۔

Anxiety often arises when we feel nervous or uneasy about uncertain situations. It's important to find ways to calm and center yourself.
