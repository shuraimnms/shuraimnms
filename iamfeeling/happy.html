Dua for Happiness / خوشی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ السُّعَدَاءِ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالآخِرَةِ

"Allahumma aj‘alni mina as-sa‘ada’i fi ad-dunya wal-akhirah."

Translation: "O Allah, make me among the happy ones in this world and the Hereafter."

Explanation: This supplication is a beautiful way to ask Allah for both worldly and eternal happiness. True happiness comes from contentment and the remembrance of Allah.

Hadith Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) taught that true happiness is found in faith and reliance on Allah. (Hisnul Muslim)
