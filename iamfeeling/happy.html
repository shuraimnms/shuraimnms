Dua for Happiness / خوشی کے لئے دعا

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ سَعَادَةً فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ

"Allahumma inni as’alu ka sa’adatan fi dunya wal akhirah"

Translation: "O Allah, I ask You for happiness in this world and the Hereafter."

Hadith Name: The Dua for Happiness
Hadith Number: Sahih Muslim 2722
Reference: The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said: "Whoever asks Allah for happiness in this world and the Hereafter, Allah will grant him success." (Sahih Muslim)
