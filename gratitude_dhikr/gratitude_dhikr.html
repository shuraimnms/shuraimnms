Gratitude Dhikr

Dua for Praising Allah in Abundance

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ عَدَدَ خَلْقِهِ، وَرِضَا نَفْسِهِ، وَزِنَةَ عَرْشِهِ وَمِدَادَ كَلِمَاتِهِ.

Subḥānallāhi wa biḥamdihi, ʿadada khalqihi, wa riḍā nafsihi, wa zinata ʿarshihi wa midāda kalimātihi.

"Glory is to Allah, and praise is to Him, equal to the number of His creation, and equal to the pleasure of His Self, and equal to the weight of His Throne, and equal to the ink of His words."

Reference: Sahih Muslim 2694

Dua for Praising Allah

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ.
(3X)

Subḥānallāhi wa biḥamdihi.

"Glory is to Allah, and praise is to Him."

Reference: Sahih Muslim 2694

Dua for Seeking Allah's Help and Guidance

يَاحَيُّ، يَا قَيُّومُ، بِرَحْمَتِكَ أَسْتَغِيثُ، أَصْلِحْ لِي شَأْنِي كُلَّهُ، وَلَا تَكِلْنِي إِلَى نَفْسِي طَرْفَةَ عَيْنٍ.

Yā ḥayyū, yā qayyūm, birahmatika astaghīth, aṣliḥ lī shāni kulla-hu, wa lā tukilnī ilā nafsī ṭarfata ʿaynin.

"O Ever-Living, O Sustainer of existence, I seek Your help with Your mercy. Set right for me all of my affairs, and do not leave me to myself for the blink of an eye."

Reference: Tirmidhi 3502

Dua for Acknowledging Allah's Oneness and Power

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ، وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ.

Lā ilāha illā Allāhu waḥdahu lā sharīka lahu, lahu al-mulku walahu al-ḥamdu wa huwa ʿalā kulli shay’in qadīr.

"There is no god but Allah, alone, with no partner. To Him belongs the dominion and to Him belongs all praise, and He has power over everything."

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6349, Sahih Muslim 2696

Dua for Contentment with Allah, Islam, and the Prophet

رَضِيتُ باللَّهِ رَبًّا، وَبِالْإِسْلَامِ دِيناً، وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ صَلَى اللَّهُ عَلِيهِ وَسَلَّمَ نَبِيَّاً.
(3X)

Raḍītu billāhi rabbā, wa bil-islāmi dīnā, wa bi Muḥammadin ṣallallāhu ʿalayhi wa sallam nabiyyā.

"I am pleased with Allah as my Lord, with Islam as my religion, and with Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him) as my Prophet."

Reference: Abu Dawood 1529, Tirmidhi 3389

Dua for Praising Allah's Greatness

اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ
(3X)

Allāhu akbar

"Allah is the greatest"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 4204

Dua for Expressing Gratitude to Allah

أَلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
(3X)

Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbil-‘ālamīn

"All praise is due to Allah, the Lord of all the worlds"

Reference: Surah Al-Fatihah 1:2

Dua for Acknowledging Allah's Favors

أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ
(3X)

Ash-hadu an lā ilāha illā Allāhu waḥdahu lā sharīka lahu

"I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, alone, with no partner"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6339

Dua for Seeking Allah's Forgiveness

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ رَبِّي مِنْ كُلِّ ذَنْبٍ أَذْنَبْتُهُ عَمَدًا أَوْ خَطَأً
(3X)

Astaghfirullāha rabbī min kulli dhanbin adhnabtuhu ‘amdan aw khaṭa’an

"I seek forgiveness from Allah, my Lord, for every sin I have committed intentionally or unintentionally"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6307

Dua for Expressing Gratitude for Allah's Mercy

رَبِّ أَشْكُرُكَ لِمَا أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيَّ
(3X)

Rabbī ashkuruka limā an‘amta ‘alayy

"My Lord, I thank You for the blessings You have bestowed upon me"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6344

Dua for Acknowledging Allah's Power and Wisdom

لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ
(3X)

Lā ḥawla wa lā quwwata illā billāhi al-‘aliyyi al-‘aẓīm

"There is no power or strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Most Great"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6384

Dua for Seeking Allah's Protection

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْ شَرِّ نَفْسِي وَمِنْ شَرِّ الشَّيْطَانِ
(3X)

A‘ūdhu billāhi min sharri nafsī wa min sharri ash-shayṭān

"I seek refuge in Allah from the evil of my own self and from the evil of Satan"

Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari 6385

Dua for Expressing Gratitude for Allah's Guidance

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي هَدَانَا لِهَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَهْتَدِيَ لَوْلَا أَنْ هَدَانَا اللَّهُ
(3X)

Al-ḥamdu lillāhi alladhī hadānā lihādhā wa mā kunnā linahtadiya lawlā an hadānā Allāh

"All praise is due to Allah, Who guided us to this, and we would not have been guided if Allah had not guided us"

Reference: Surah Al-A‘raf 7:43
