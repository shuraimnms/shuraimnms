Steps to Perform Ghusl for Ladies

  • Step 1: Make the intention (Niyyah) in your heart for purification.
  • Step 2: Begin by washing both hands thoroughly up to the wrists.
  • Step 3: Perform Wudu (ablution) as you would for Salah, but delay washing the feet until the end.
  • Step 4: Pour water over your head three times, ensuring that the water reaches the roots of your hair.
  • Step 5: Wash the entire body thoroughly, starting from the right side and then the left, ensuring that no part of the body is left dry.
  • Step 6: Finally, wash both feet, making sure to clean between the toes.
  • Note: During Ghusl, ensure complete modesty and privacy. This is an act of worship and purification.
