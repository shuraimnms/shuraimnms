How to Perform Istikhara استخارہ کیسے کریں

Steps to Perform Istikhara

  • Step 1: Begin by making Niyyah (intention) for Istikhara prayer. Perform Wudu (ablution) if necessary.
  • Step 2: Pray two Rak'ahs of voluntary prayer (Nafl prayer) after which you make Dua for guidance from Allah regarding your decision.
  • Step 3: Recite the Istikhara dua after completing the two Rak'ahs. The dua is as follows:
    اللهم إني استخيرك بعلمك واستقدرك بقدرتك، واسالك من فضلك العظيم
  • Step 4: Trust Allah’s decision. After performing Istikhara, rely on Allah’s guidance and take action accordingly based on the feelings and circumstances that arise.
  • Step 5: If you feel confused, do not worry. Istikhara will bring clarity, and you should look for signs that guide your decision.
  • Step 6: Istikhara can be repeated multiple times if needed. Allah's guidance may come in the form of a feeling or a circumstance that helps you decide.
