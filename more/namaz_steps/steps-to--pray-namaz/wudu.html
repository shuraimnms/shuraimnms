How to Perform Wudu وضو کرنے کا طریقہ

Steps to Perform Wudu

  • Step 1: Begin with the Niyyah (intention) for Wudu.
  • Step 2: Say 'Bismillah' before starting.
  • Step 3: Wash your hands up to the wrists three times.
  • Step 4: Rinse your mouth three times, ensuring cleanliness.
  • Step 5: Clean your nose by inhaling water three times and blowing it out.
  • Step 6: Wash your face completely three times.
  • Step 7: Wash your arms up to the elbows three times, starting with the right arm.
  • Step 8: Wipe your head (Masah) once, including your ears.
  • Step 9: Wash your feet up to the ankles three times, starting with the right foot.
  • Step 10: Recite the Shahada: 'Ashhadu an la ilaha illa Allah, wa ashhadu anna Muhammadur Rasulullah.'
