Steps to Perform Tahajjud Prayer

  • Step 1: Begin by making Niyyah (intention) for Tahajjud prayer. Perform Wudu (ablution) if necessary.
  • Step 2: Ensure you wake up in the last part of the night, preferably after midnight.
  • Step 3: Begin your Tahajjud prayer by offering at least 2 Rak'ahs as a minimum.
  • Step 4: After the initial 2 Rak'ahs, you may offer additional Rak'ahs in sets of two.
  • Step 5: Recite Surah Al-Fatiha followed by another Surah in each Rak'ah. The longer and more heartfelt, the better.
  • Step 6: The last part of your prayer should be an earnest Dua, asking Allah for forgiveness, guidance, and blessings.
  • Step 7: End your prayer with Tasleem (saying Salam) after completing the final Rak'ah.
  • Step 8: You may also offer a two-Rak'ah Witr prayer at the end of your Tahajjud night.
