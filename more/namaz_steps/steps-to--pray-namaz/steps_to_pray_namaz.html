Steps to Perform Namaz

  • Step 1: Begin by making Niyyah (intention) for the prayer.
  • Step 2: Stand in Qiyam (standing position) and recite the opening Takbeer (Allahu Akbar).
  • Step 3: Recite Surah Al-Fatiha followed by another Surah of your choice.
  • Step 4: Perform Ruku (bowing) and say Subhana Rabbiyal Azeem.
  • Step 5: Stand again in the upright position and say Sami Allahu liman hamidah.
  • Step 6: Perform Sujud (prostration) and say Subhana Rabbiyal A'la.
  • Step 7: Sit in between the two Sujuds and say Rabbighfir li.
  • Step 8: Complete the prayer with the Tashahhud (testification of faith) and Tasleem (salutation).
