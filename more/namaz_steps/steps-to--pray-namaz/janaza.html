How to Perform Janaza Prayer جنازہ کی نماز کیسے پڑھیں

Steps to Perform Janaza Prayer

  • Step 1: Stand in rows behind the Imam, ensuring the body of the deceased is in front of the congregation. The head of a male or the middle of a female should face the Imam.
  • Step 2: Make an intention (niyyah) in your heart to perform the Janaza prayer for the deceased for the sake of Allah.
  • Step 3: The Janaza prayer consists of **four Takbirs** (saying "Allahu Akbar"):
    • First Takbir: After the first Takbir, recite Surah Al-Fatiha (starting with "Bismillah...").
    • Second Takbir: After the second Takbir, send blessings upon the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) by reciting "Allahumma salli ala Muhammadin..." (Durood Ibrahim).
    • Third Takbir: After the third Takbir, make Dua for the deceased. You can recite a specific supplication like:

      "Allahumma aghfir li hayyina wa mayyitina wa shahidina wa gha'ibina wa saghirina wa kabirina wa dhakarina wa unthana..."

    • Fourth Takbir: After the fourth Takbir, make a short Dua and then conclude the prayer with Tasleem (saying "As-Salaamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullah" to the right and left).
  • Step 4: Pray with sincerity, seeking Allah’s mercy and forgiveness for the deceased and all Muslims.
  • Step 5: After completing the prayer, assist with the burial if possible, as it is part of the rewardable act in Islam.
