Your Complete Guide to Namaz, Wudu, Ghusl, and More

Namaz Steps نماز کے مراحل

Learn step-by-step how to perform Salah with correct actions and recitations.

  • Takbeer (Allahu Akbar)
  • Qiyam (Standing)
  • Ruku (Bowing)
  • Sujood (Prostration)
  • Tashahhud (Sitting)
  • Salam (Ending)

How to Perform Wudu وضو کیسے کریں

A step-by-step guide to purification before prayer.

  • Wash Hands
  • Rinse Mouth
  • Wash Face
  • Wipe Head
  • Wash Feet

How to Pray Tahajjud تہجد کی نماز کیسے پڑھیں

Learn the best way to offer Tahajjud prayer during the night.

  • Perform Wudu
  • Stand in Qiyam and recite Quran
  • Perform 2 Rakats at a time

How to Perform Ghusl غسل کیسے کریں

A complete guide to the purification bath (Ghusl).

  • Make Intention
  • Wash Hands
  • Wash Private Parts
  • Pour Water Over Entire Body

How to Perform Istikhara استخارہ کیسے کریں

A guide to seeking guidance from Allah for decisions.

  • Pray 2 Rakats
  • Recite Istikhara Dua
  • Place Trust in Allah
